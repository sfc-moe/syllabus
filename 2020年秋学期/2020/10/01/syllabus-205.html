科目概要

This lecture is aimed at students with little prior knowledge about the European Union. After an overview of European integration from the European Coal and Steel Community (ECSC, 1951) to today’s European Union, the EU’s main institutions and political stakeholders will be examined. How are the European commission, the European Council, the European Parliament or the European Court of Justice set up? What are their powers and competences, and what is the process of creating European policies and laws? The lecture will then address the workings of the European Union considering specific policy fields like foreign policy, security policy or agriculture policy, as well as the handling of specific problem situations like the financial crisis or the refugee crisis, before finally looking at various options for future developments.

 

授業シラバス

主題と目標／授業の手法など

Key goals of the course are to understand the historic developments and preconditions of today’s challenges in EU, being able to analyze recent conflicts and front lines and to develop a capability to identify spheres of influences and national interests.Besides,there will be a strong focus on students' presentation skills.

Teaching methods are lecture, pair/group work, students' presentations, reading assignemnts, writing essays.

第2回 From WW II to EU (European Union)

 

The initiation of European integration: From the rubbles of WW II to European Coal and Steel Community (1951), Treaty of Rome (1957) and Single European Act (1986)

 

 

第3回 EU Treaties

 

European integration from the Treaty of Maastricht (1998) to the Treaty of Lisbon (2007)

 

 

第4回 EU’s political system 1

 

European Council, European Commission, European Parliament, The System of Multi-Level-Governance, The Implementation of European policies

 

 

第5回 EU’s political system 2

 

Europ. Court of Justice, Europ. Central Bank, Europ. Court of Audition

 

 

第6回 EU Policies 1

 

EU’s economic and agricultural policies; EU’s environmental and social policies

 

 

第7回 EU Policies 2

 

EU foreign and security policy; EU and its neighbors; Relations to Russia and Ukraine; Turkey`s EU-candidacy

 

 

第8回 EU Crises 1

 

Corona-Epidemic; Economic and Financial Crisis

 

 

第9回 EU Crises 2

 

Refugee crisis and EU migration policy

 

 

第10回 EU challenges 1

 

New nationalism and dissolution tendencies (e.g. Brexit); Authoritarian tendencies and conflicts with national policies & EU law

 

 

第11回 EU challenges 2

 

Political and economic spheres of influence

 

 

第12回 Conclusion
