科目概要

This course aims at introducing students to the contemporary Indonesia. It will cover the recent changes in Indonesian society, popular culture and sub-cultures, and business to some extent. A guest lecture will be organized to provide students with current information.

授業シラバス

主題と目標／授業の手法など

Objectives:
1. The class provides a general idea of contemporary Indonesia.
2. The class provides first hand knowledge about understanding Indonesian's society.

Teaching Methods:
The class will be conducted in short lectures and discussions. Students are expected to read the material before class in order to answer discussion questions. Students are also expected to prepare presentations.

教材・参考文献

print out or pdf will be distributed in class.

提出課題・試験・成績評価の方法など
