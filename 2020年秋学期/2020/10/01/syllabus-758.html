授業シラバス

主題と目標／授業の手法など

The Marketing and Advertising Secrets of International Health and Wellness Companies


Type:Writing/Presentation

Keyword:Business English,Discussion,Presentation,Project,Speaking,Web Activities,Marketing

Course Description:

This is an active class. You will learn about the marketing and advertising strategies of manywell known international health and wellness companies and organizations in fields such as: 3D printing human hands, medical tourism, seniorcare, cosmetic surgery clinics, and NPO/NGOs. You will also do a real world marketing projecttodistribute clean water filters forWaves for Water. While you learn about marketing, you will also improve your writing and presentation skills.

Below is a short video about Waves for Water

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1zRmofkyVis&nohtml5=False

Below is a short video about Project Daniel - 3D printing human hands

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SDYFMgrjeLg

We will use the following technology to help us with our class: 3D printers, mobile phones, digital cameras, and the Internet.

***Please understand this class has a large amount of groupwork and you must come to class prepared. If you do not like groupwork and you do not have the time to prepare before class, this class is NOT a good matchfor you***



Learning outcomes:

After completing this course, you will know about the marketing and advertising used by many well known internatioanl health and wellness companies and NPO/NGOs. You will understand and be able to use the 4P's of marketing. You will also have communicated with classmates in small teams and learned how to use 3D printers. You will have improved your writing and presentation skills.



Teacher's comment:

If you have any questions about how you are evaluated, please ask me immediately. I will be happy to explain to you in more detail what you can do to achieve a higher score.

I'm very involvedoutside of class withmarketing and advertising. If you have any questions, I'm always happy to help! I look forward to seeing you in class.
